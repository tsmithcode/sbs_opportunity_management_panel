# Premium Enterprise Buyer Packet

## Purpose
This document is the buyer-facing entrypoint for Monyawn premium enterprise review.

It is designed for:
- procurement reviewers
- security and privacy reviewers
- executive sponsors
- solutions and implementation stakeholders

It should be shared as the first concise packet before deeper diligence materials are requested.

## Executive Summary
Monyawn is a local-first, AI-native opportunity workflow platform designed to help people and organizations move through guided opportunity workflows with stronger clarity, reviewable AI assistance, and durable handoff packaging.

The current product posture is:
- local-first
- export-first
- human-reviewable
- multi-opportunity by default
- not dependent on Monyawn-hosted user opportunity-data retention for standard use

Monyawn should not currently be represented as:
- a standard hosted SaaS with company-side retention of user opportunity data
- a sovereign-hosted platform
- a region-guaranteed residency product
- a certified compliance platform unless explicitly documented

## What Buyers Should Understand First
- Monyawn is strong in workflow clarity, handoff packaging, and local data control.
- The current product prioritizes user-controlled data custody over company-side data retention.
- AI usage is governed, reviewable, and bounded by documented claim rules.
- Sovereign, residency-bound, or non-standard hosting questions require controlled review and are not default product claims.

## What Monyawn Offers Today
- guided opportunity workflow system
- candidate story and coaching support
- structured extraction and reviewable outputs
- ZIP export/import with canonical JSON plus human-readable Markdown and PDF artifacts
- trust, governance, and readiness documentation for early premium enterprise review

## Current Product Boundaries
- no Monyawn-hosted user opportunity-data persistence in the current local-first product model
- no documented sovereign hosting offer
- no documented region-bound hosted residency offer
- no unsupported certification or compliance claims
