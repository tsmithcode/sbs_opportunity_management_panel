# Premium Enterprise Pursuit Criteria

## Purpose
This document defines when Monyawn should actively pursue a premium enterprise opportunity versus when it should slow, qualify more deeply, or decline.

## Pursuit Principles
- do not chase deals that force unsupported commitments
- prefer credibility over premature enterprise revenue
- qualify against product truth, supportability, and controlled-domain readiness

## Positive Pursuit Signals
- buyer understands the current local-first posture
- buyer values export-first and user-controlled data custody
- diligence questions can be answered from current docs or controlled review
- requested support model fits documented operations
- commercial expectations fit documented approval ranges
- requested deployment posture does not require unsupported sovereignty or hosting claims

## Slow-Down Signals
- buyer expects commitments not yet documented
- deal depends on custom hosted posture that is not currently offered
- support expectations outpace current operating model
- legal or procurement requirements need heavy redlines
- pricing expectations depend on speculative roadmap or non-standard promises

## Decline Signals
- buyer requires immediate commitments to unsupported sovereign or residency guarantees
- buyer requires undocumented compliance or certification claims
- buyer requires contract acceptance outside legal and executive appetite
- buyer expects product behavior that contradicts the current implementation

## Qualification Questions
- Does the buyer accept the current local-first posture?
- Does the buyer understand that Monyawn does not currently retain user opportunity data on company systems in the local-first product?
- Are requested diligence answers supported by current documents?
- Are requested pricing and support terms within documented approval posture?
- Does the deal depend on unsupported hosted, sovereign, or regulated-environment commitments?

## Ownership
Primary owners:
- CRO / Revenue Lead
- President / GM

Supporting owners:
- Deal Desk / Commercial Operations Lead
- Enterprise Counsel / Contracting Lead
- Customer Assurance / Security Questionnaire Lead
- Data Residency / Sovereignty Lead

## Summary
The best premium enterprise opportunities are the ones Monyawn can pursue without compromising product truth, trust posture, or controlled-domain discipline.
