# Premium Enterprise Approved Answer Bank

## Purpose
This document gives staff and AI a set of short, approved answer patterns for common premium enterprise questions.

These answers are intentionally conservative. If a question goes beyond these boundaries, route it.

## Approved Answers

### What is Monyawn?
"Monyawn is a local-first, AI-native opportunity workflow platform. In the current product model, it helps users manage opportunity workflows, generate reviewable outputs, and export durable handoff packages without relying on Monyawn-hosted user opportunity data storage for normal use."

### Do you store our opportunity data on your servers?
"In the current local-first product model, user opportunity data remains on the user-controlled device unless the user exports it. Monyawn does not retain that opportunity data on company systems in the current version."

### Are you a SaaS platform today?
"The current product posture is local-first rather than a traditional hosted SaaS with Monyawn-side user opportunity data retention."

### Do you support sovereign hosting?
"We should not imply sovereign hosting as a current product capability. The current version is local-first. Sovereign deployment questions require formal review by the appropriate architecture, privacy, security, and contracting owners."

### Do you guarantee data residency in our jurisdiction?
"We should not make that claim in the current product model. The current version is local-first, and hosted regional residency commitments are not part of the documented offer."

### Are you compliant with a specific standard?
"We only claim compliance, certification, or conformance where it is explicitly documented. If you need a specific representation, we would route that for formal review."

### How do you handle AI risk?
"Monyawn uses documented AI governance with human review gates, controlled claim boundaries, and escalation rules. We should stay within our published AI governance materials and avoid implying controls that are not documented."

### How should we understand your export and retention model?
"The current product uses local persistence for convenience and ZIP export for durable handoff. Canonical session restoration comes from `session.json`, while Markdown and PDF artifacts are human-readable derivatives."

### Can you sign our custom terms?
"Contract and redline requests require commercial and legal review. We do not approve custom terms through informal conversation."

## Escalation Triggers
Route immediately if the buyer asks for:
- sovereign hosting commitments
- residency guarantees
- certifications or audits not documented
- contract acceptance
- price exceptions
- regulated-environment claims

## Summary
The approved answer bank is meant to keep premium conversations calm, credible, and exact. If the exact answer is not documented, the correct answer is to route it.
