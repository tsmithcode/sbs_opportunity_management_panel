# Town Hall And Phase Vote

## Purpose
Document the executive town hall used to align Monyawn leadership on the next phased execution plan for enterprise-grade operations, premium governance, and premium workflow UX.

## Executive Anchors
- CEO
- President / GM
- People / HR Lead

## Required Briefs

### CEO Brief
- Why Monyawn is becoming an enterprise-grade opportunity platform rather than remaining a demo wizard
- Why governance, accessibility, and trust must scale with product ambition
- Why the company needs platform-ops maturity before stronger enterprise claims

### President / GM Brief
- Recommended operating-model decision
- Revenue impact of Platform Ops First versus UI-first sequencing
- Tradeoffs across product, delivery, and enterprise readiness

### HR Brief
- Functional org expansion needed for enterprise execution
- Which roles may be fractional, partner-supported, or combined temporarily
- Team-health and execution risks if operations are under-staffed

### Product And Engineering Brief
- Exact gaps between current app shell and end-to-end platform readiness
- CRUD, pipeline, import/export, document, correspondence, and governance gaps
- Delivery sequence for turning the local platform shell into a real product system

### Governance Brief
- Why human review cannot be bypassed for high-stakes outputs
- Why enterprise premium governance must exist before strong external claims
- How policy overlays, escalation routing, and document lifecycle controls fit together

## Recommended Vote Motion
Approve the next phase as:
- Platform Ops First
- Human-in-the-loop as the operating model
- Premium UI and accessibility standards enforced on every shipped surface
- Enterprise-complete functional org coverage, with fractional staffing allowed where appropriate

## Recommended Vote Outcome
The recommended vote outcome is:
- Approved: Platform Ops First
- Approved: human-in-the-loop governance and data model
- Approved: premium accessibility and premium product UX as a required standard, not a substitute for operations

## Phase Decision

### Phase 2A: Enterprise Ops Backbone
- persistence and canonical entities
- CRUD for operational records
- stage events, AI checkpoints, review tasks, and escalation logging
- onboarding intake, artifact upload, candidate profile confirmation, and save/resume
- staff queues for review, correction, and escalation
- API and import/export contracts
- document management and correspondence foundations

### Phase 2B: Premium Workflow Surfaces
- real forms and live workflow state
- progressive disclosure across user, reviewer, and admin modes
- premium accessibility execution
- evidence, confidence, and review-gate UI

### Phase 2C: Governance And Enterprise Readiness
- policy-to-runtime enforcement
- account-type and region-sensitive overlays
- reporting, trust-center alignment, support routing, and buyer-facing operational readiness

## Follow-Through
After the vote:
- update org authority docs
- update platform roadmap
- assign owners for each phase
- publish the vote outcome as a source of truth for the AI and staff
