# Risk Register

| Risk | Severity | Why It Matters | Mitigation |
|---|---:|---|---|
| Revit API evidence is thin in the supplied resume | High | The posting explicitly names Revit as a strong plus | Position Autodesk breadth honestly; emphasize fast API ramp patterns; study Revit-specific concepts before interviews |
| Role may be leveled below current capability | High | Posting says mid-level, 3–6 years; your document reads senior/architect | Decide whether title matters less than fit, pay, and trajectory; ask calibrated questions later in process |
| Utility/substation domain is not explicit in resume | Medium | SBS serves utilities and critical infrastructure | Translate manufacturing/CAD/system correctness experience into infrastructure-grade rigor |
| Resume may feel too enterprise/architectural for a feature-delivery role | Medium | Team may want hands-on implementation more than strategy | Bring examples of coding, debugging, desktop app delivery, geometry/data integrity work |
| Compensation band may cap upside | Medium | Posted base range is $140k–$160k + up to 10% bonus | Enter process only if total package, growth, and role scope justify it |
| Over-positioning as a pure architect could reduce fit | Medium | Team wants feature design and implementation | Use builder language: shipped, debugged, implemented, integrated, stabilized |
