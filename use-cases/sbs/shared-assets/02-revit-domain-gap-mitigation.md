# Revit and Domain Gap Mitigation Plan

## Goal
Reduce perceived risk without claiming experience you do not have.

## Messaging Strategy
- State Autodesk depth honestly.
- Frame Revit as adjacent ramp, not a fabricated competency.
- Emphasize past success learning domain-bound software systems quickly.

## Practical Prep Areas
### Revit-side
- document/object/element model basics
- transactions
- families and parameters
- model relationships
- common add-in patterns
- performance pitfalls

### Domain-side
- electrical substation basics
- equipment/layout concepts
- why correctness matters in utility infrastructure
- common constraints in critical infrastructure design tools

## Interview Phrase
"My direct evidence is strongest in Inventor, AutoCAD, and Vault, but I have repeatedly succeeded in Autodesk-adjacent environments by learning the domain model quickly and then building reliable software around real engineering workflows."
