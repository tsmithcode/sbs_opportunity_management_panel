# Question Bank — Practice Prompts

## Recruiter
- Walk me through your background.
- Why this SBS role?
- What is your experience with Autodesk platforms?
- How close are you to the compensation range?
- What would make you leave your current path for this role?

## Hiring Manager
- Which parts of your background are most relevant to our Design Tools team?
- How do you approach building software for domain experts?
- How have you handled platform migrations or Autodesk upgrades?
- What is your experience with performance-sensitive engineering tools?
- How do you balance correctness and speed?

## Technical
- How would you structure a desktop application integrating with external design APIs?
- How would you debug intermittent model corruption or data integrity issues?
- How would you improve performance in a geometry-heavy workflow?
- How would you make a complex engineering feature testable?
- How would you design observability for user-reported design inconsistencies?

## Behavioral
- Tell me about a time you had to influence without authority.
- Tell me about a time you learned a new technical domain quickly.
- Tell me about a time a deployment or upgrade went wrong.
- Tell me about a time you improved developer workflow or standards.
