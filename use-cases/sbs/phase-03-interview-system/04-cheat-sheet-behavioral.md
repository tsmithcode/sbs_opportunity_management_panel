# Interview Cheat Sheet — Behavioral / Collaboration

## Themes to Reinforce
- calm under ambiguity
- accountable problem solving
- cross-functional communication
- respect for domain experts
- technical rigor without ego
- measurable delivery

## STAR Story Inventory
### 1. Platform modernization under constraint
Situation: legacy Autodesk add-ins / upgrade pressure  
Task: modernize safely  
Action: migrate, stabilize, control rollout  
Result: improved maintainability and readiness

### 2. Workflow quality improvement
Situation: poor drawing-request inputs and clarification loops  
Task: improve throughput and request quality  
Action: standardize input/data extraction  
Result: 12–14 drawings/day and 60% fewer clarification loops

### 3. Cost/governance improvement
Situation: inefficient Autodesk licensing  
Task: optimize cost and usage discipline  
Action: token model + SOPs + training + reporting  
Result: 24% lower cost, 80% fewer recurring issues

## Behavioral Questions to Expect
- Tell me about a time you had to learn a complex domain quickly.
- Tell me about a time you disagreed with a technical approach.
- Tell me about a hard bug and how you isolated it.
- Tell me about a time you improved a system without full authority.
- Tell me about a time you worked with non-software experts.

## Tone Guidance
- direct
- concrete
- measurable
- not theatrical
