# Interview Process Map

## Expected Sequence
1. recruiter / talent screen
2. hiring manager screen
3. technical interview
4. team / panel interview
5. final alignment and offer discussion

## Preparation By Stage
### Stage 1
Bring fit, motivation, comp calibration, and concise background.

### Stage 2
Bring relevant stories and role alignment, especially Autodesk + desktop + engineering tools.

### Stage 3
Bring implementation depth, debugging method, architecture thinking, and honesty around gaps.

### Stage 4
Bring collaboration, domain learning mindset, and clear communication.

### Stage 5
Bring decision criteria and negotiation posture.

## Between-Round Workflow
- write debrief notes immediately
- record names, roles, concerns, and open questions
- update the tracker
- send thank-you note within 24 hours
- refresh next-round cheat sheet
