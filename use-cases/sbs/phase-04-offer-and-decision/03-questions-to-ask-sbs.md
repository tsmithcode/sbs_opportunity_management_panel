# Questions to Ask SBS

## Early Stage
- How is the Design Tools team structured?
- What are the primary Autodesk platforms or internal systems this role touches?
- How much of the work is net-new feature development versus maintenance and modernization?

## Hiring Manager Stage
- What are the hardest technical problems the team is solving right now?
- What does success look like in the first 90 days?
- How much interaction does this role have with domain experts and product leadership?
- How important is direct Revit API depth relative to adjacent Autodesk experience?

## Late Stage
- How is level determined on this team?
- What growth path exists from this role?
- How are strong performers recognized and advanced?
