# Premium Enterprise Positioning And Claims Guide

## Purpose
This document defines the approved premium enterprise positioning for Monyawn and the claim boundaries that must be preserved in README, trust materials, buyer conversations, and AI-generated company statements.

## Positioning Statement
Monyawn is a local-first, AI-native opportunity operating system designed to help people and organizations move through opportunity workflows with stronger clarity, narrative quality, and durable handoff packaging without storing user opportunity data on Monyawn systems in the current product model.

## What Monyawn Is Today
- a local-first workflow platform
- a multi-opportunity workspace with one actively selected opportunity at a time
- an AI-assisted system for candidate story, coaching, structured extraction, and workflow guidance
- an export-first product with ZIP, JSON, Markdown, and PDF handoff outputs
- a product with user, staff, and admin operating views
- a platform with trust, governance, and readiness documentation suitable for early premium enterprise diligence

## What Monyawn Is Not Yet
- a cloud-sync product
- a Monyawn-hosted multi-tenant SaaS with company-side data retention
- a formally certified sovereign hosting platform
- a product that should imply region-specific residency or regulated hosting commitments without documented support

## Premium Enterprise Narrative Rules
- Lead with truth before ambition.
- Present local-only and export-first as strengths, not as apologetic limitations.
- Treat sovereignty, residency, and hosted enterprise posture as controlled subjects, not as default marketing language.
- Keep product claims aligned to live implementation and approved docs.
- Differentiate current capability from future direction every time hosted or sovereign questions appear.

## Approved Narrative Themes
- user dignity through local control
- executive-grade workflow clarity
- durable handoff packaging without hidden custody
- guided, reviewable AI assistance instead of opaque automation
- premium operational structure without fabricated compliance claims

## Prohibited Or Escalation-Only Claims
- "We are sovereign-hosted"
- "We guarantee regional data residency"
- "We are compliant with" unless documented
- "We are certified for" unless documented
- "We support enterprise hosting in your required jurisdiction" unless documented
- any claim that changes the current local-only posture without approved architecture, legal, privacy, and security review

## Review Cadence
- README and root narrative review: monthly or when major product posture changes
- trust-center and buyer-facing claims review: monthly or before major buyer conversations
- sovereign and residency language review: before any premium enterprise diligence cycle
- AI company-statement review: whenever new claim categories are introduced

## Audience Guidance
- Builders need truthful implementation boundaries first
- Buyers need confidence, control, and escalation clarity
- Users need plain-language benefit and data-custody clarity

## Summary
Premium enterprise positioning for Monyawn should feel disciplined, calm, and credible. The goal is not to sound bigger than the product. The goal is to sound exact, trustworthy, and executive-ready.
