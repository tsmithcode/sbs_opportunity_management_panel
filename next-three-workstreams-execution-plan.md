# Next Three Workstreams Execution Plan

## Purpose
This document defines the next three premium-enterprise workstreams Monyawn should execute in order and the expert panel required to complete them well.

## Ordered Workstreams

| Sequence | Workstream | Goal | Primary Owners | Required Supporting Experts | Expected Outcome |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Diligence packet export | Turn the buyer-facing docs into a reusable send-order packet with audience-specific guidance | Trust Center / Public Documentation Owner, Customer Assurance / Security Questionnaire Lead | Repository Steward / Documentation Systems Lead, CRO / Revenue Lead, Privacy Lead, Security Lead, PDF / Document Rendering Specialist | Staff can send a clean, repeatable diligence packet without rebuilding it each time |
| 2 | Premium enterprise slide deck | Turn the product, trust, and buyer story into an executive-grade presentation for sponsors and buying committees | Brand Strategy Lead, Marketing / Communications Lead | Mission / Purpose Lead, CRO / Revenue Lead, Trust Center / Public Documentation Owner, Executive Presentation Lead, UX Writer / Content Designer | Monyawn has a premium sponsor-ready deck that matches the buyer packet and trust posture |
| 3 | Enterprise-facing product and admin controls | Add the product/admin implementation layer needed for enterprise-facing workflows, governance visibility, and controlled operations | Product Lead / AI Product Architect, Engineering Lead | Enterprise Product Operations Lead, Entitlements And Admin Controls Lead, Accessibility Lead, Security Lead, Privacy Lead, QA / Reliability Lead, Solutions Architect | The product begins to support enterprise-facing administration and governance controls instead of docs alone carrying the burden |

## Required Expert Panel

| Expert | Why Required | Core Ownership |
|---|---|---|
| Diligence Packet / Sales Enablement Lead | Needed to turn trust and buyer docs into a repeatable external packet flow | Packet assembly, send order, audience packaging, handoff discipline |
| Executive Presentation Lead | Needed to convert premium buyer materials into a credible sponsor-facing deck | Executive deck structure, meeting narrative, presentation discipline |
| Enterprise Product Operations Lead | Needed to translate enterprise buyer expectations into controllable product operations | Enterprise workflow operations, admin-facing requirements, governance operating fit |
| Entitlements And Admin Controls Lead | Needed to design role, permission, and admin-surface expectations before implementation drifts | Admin controls, role visibility, governed enterprise access patterns |
| Solutions Demo Lead | Needed to ensure the deck, packet, and product walkthrough tell the same truth in buyer conversations | Demo narrative, proof flow, buyer walkthrough coherence |

## Sequence Rule
- Do not start the slide deck before the diligence packet export flow is defined.
- Do not start enterprise-facing admin/control implementation before the buyer packet and deck are aligned.
- Keep all three workstreams tied to current product truth, not aspirational enterprise language.

## Summary
These three workstreams should be done in order because each one depends on the previous one being coherent: packet first, deck second, enterprise-facing product controls third.
