# Support Policy

## Purpose
This document defines the default support posture for Monyawn at the platform level.

It is an operational policy draft and should be refined into customer-facing support commitments, SLAs, or contract terms as the business matures.

## Support Principles
- provide clear support paths
- prioritize blocking issues appropriately
- communicate responsibly during incidents or service degradation
- distinguish between product guidance, technical issues, and account issues

## Support Scope
Support may include:
- onboarding assistance
- workflow questions
- account access issues
- technical defect reports
- accessibility issue reports
- enterprise implementation support

## Support Channels
Support channels may vary by product tier, account type, and contract.

The company should maintain a current internal and customer-facing record of:
- support contact methods
- support hours
- escalation path
- severity definitions

## Severity Expectations
The support model should distinguish between:
- informational questions
- normal product issues
- blocking workflow issues
- security or privacy incidents
- accessibility barriers

## Escalation
Support issues should be escalated to the responsible function when needed, including:
- engineering
- customer success
- accessibility
- security
- privacy
- finance

## Governance
Primary owners:
- Customer Success Lead
- Operations Lead

Consulted owners:
- Engineering Lead
- Accessibility Lead
- Security Lead
- Privacy Lead

## Summary
Monyawn should give users and buyers a clear path to help, escalation, and status visibility. If support expectations are not documented, the AI and staff should avoid inventing commitments.
