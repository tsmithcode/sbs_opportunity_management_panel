# Deployment Commitment Review Rule

## Purpose
This document defines when deployment-related questions and requests must be reviewed before Monyawn says yes, implies support, or accepts a related commercial term.

## Review Triggers
Review is required when a buyer asks for:
- hosted deployment by Monyawn
- region-specific or residency-bound hosting
- sovereign or isolated environment commitments
- security, privacy, or compliance implications tied to deployment shape
- environment-specific support or availability promises
- architecture diagrams or deployment descriptions beyond current documented posture

## Required Review Owners
- Sovereign Deployment Architect
- Data Residency / Sovereignty Lead
- Engineering Lead
- Privacy Lead
- Security Lead
- Enterprise Counsel / Contracting Lead

## AI Rule
The AI may explain that the current product is local-first and that deployment-specific commitments require review. It must not speculate about supportability.

## Summary
Deployment commitments are controlled commitments. They should move through explicit review before they become part of a buyer conversation or commercial package.
