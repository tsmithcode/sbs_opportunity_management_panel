# Premium Enterprise One-Pager

## Monyawn
Local-first, AI-native opportunity workflow platform

## What Monyawn Does
Monyawn helps people and organizations move through opportunity workflows with:
- guided decision support
- reviewable AI assistance
- candidate story and coaching support
- structured extraction from workflow artifacts
- durable export packaging for handoff and retention

## Why It Matters
Most workflow tools force users into opaque systems or hidden data custody. Monyawn takes a different posture:
- local-first by design
- export-first for durable handoff
- human-reviewable AI assistance
- disciplined trust and claim boundaries

## Current Product Posture
- local-first
- multi-opportunity by default
- one actively selected opportunity at a time for guided clarity
- ZIP export/import with canonical JSON plus Markdown and PDF artifacts
- not dependent on Monyawn-hosted user opportunity-data retention for standard use

## What Monyawn Is Not Today
- not a standard hosted SaaS with company-side retention of user opportunity data
- not a documented sovereign-hosted offer
- not a documented region-guaranteed residency product
- not a platform that should imply certifications or compliance without explicit evidence

## Why Buyers Engage
- clearer workflow operations
- stronger user-controlled data posture
- reviewable AI behavior
- premium trust and diligence discipline without fabricated enterprise claims

## What Serious Buyers Should Know
- current product truth comes before ambition
- sovereign, residency, hosted deployment, and non-standard commitments require controlled review
- security, privacy, and AI governance answers should come from documented source materials
